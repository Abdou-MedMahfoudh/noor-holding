Nour Holding Expands Operations Across West Africa

We are excited to announce our strategic expansion into three new markets across West Africa, strengthening our commitment to regional growth and sustainable development while creating new opportunities for local communities.

NOUAKCHOTT, Mauritania - Nour Holdings, a leading investment and development company in Mauritania, today announced its ambitious expansion into three key West African markets: Senegal, Mali, and Burkina Faso. This strategic move represents a significant milestone in the company's growth trajectory and demonstrates its commitment to fostering economic development across the region.

Strategic Vision for Regional Growth

The expansion initiative, which has been in development for over two years, aligns with Nour Holdings' core mission of creating sustainable value through strategic investments and partnerships. The company will establish operational offices in Dakar, Bamako, and Ouagadougou over the next 18 months, with initial investments totaling $50 million across various sectors.

"This expansion represents more than just business growth; it's about building bridges between communities and creating opportunities that benefit everyone," said the company's leadership team. "West Africa is experiencing unprecedented economic growth, and we want to be part of that success story while bringing our expertise in sustainable development to new markets."

Focus Areas and Investment Sectors

The expansion will focus on four key sectors where Nour Holdings has established expertise and sees significant potential for impact:

  • Infrastructure Development: Supporting transportation, telecommunications, and energy projects that connect communities and facilitate economic growth.
  • Agricultural Innovation: Investing in modern farming techniques, supply chain improvements, and food security initiatives.
  • Financial Services: Expanding access to banking and microfinance services for small and medium enterprises.
  • Education and Training: Developing vocational training programs and educational infrastructure to build local capacity.

Local Partnerships and Community Impact

Central to Nour Holdings' expansion strategy is the development of strong partnerships with local businesses, government entities, and community organizations. The company has already signed memorandums of understanding with several regional partners and is actively engaging with local stakeholders to ensure that its investments create meaningful, lasting benefits for local communities.

The expansion is expected to create over 500 direct jobs and thousands of indirect employment opportunities across the three countries. Additionally, Nour Holdings will implement its proven community development programs, which include skills training, youth entrepreneurship initiatives, and women's economic empowerment projects.

Timeline and Next Steps

The phased expansion will begin in Senegal in Q4 2025, followed by Mali in Q2 2026, and Burkina Faso in Q4 2026. Each phase will include comprehensive market research, stakeholder engagement, and the establishment of local operations teams comprised primarily of regional talent.

"We're taking a measured, thoughtful approach to this expansion," the company noted. "Success in new markets requires deep understanding of local contexts, strong relationships, and a genuine commitment to adding value to the communities where we operate."

About Nour Holdings

Founded in Mauritania, Nour Holdings is a diversified investment and development company focused on creating sustainable value across multiple sectors. With a strong track record of successful investments and community development initiatives, the company is committed to fostering economic growth while maintaining the highest standards of social and environmental responsibility.
